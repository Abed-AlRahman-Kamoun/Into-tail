Company brief

Into Tail is a pet service provider in Riyadh Saudi Arabia that provides professional dog training, along with boarding services for cats and dogs, in addition to the best pet transportation services. Into tail stands out among dog trainer schools for the quality of training it provides and has all that your cat and dog need to enjoy their stay; while also being qualified for taking care of your pet travel from and to Saudi Arabia.

History

Established in Riyadh in 2010, Into tail has grown fueled by its love for pets and has become one of the best pet care providers in Saudi Arabia, leaving many clients satisfied with the attention provided to their pets.
